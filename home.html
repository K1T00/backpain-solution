The most common cause of back pain is unhealthy back muscles

  • Especially the multifidus muscles are critical.
  • Muscle atrophy and fat deposits on multifidus muscles usually leads to back pain.
  • Both issues are best prevented by training those muscles.

To effectively train those muscles the rotation of the pelvis must be prevented

  • MedX lumbar extension devices are designed to fixate the pelvis during training (see video or video).
  • Unfortunately, these devices are very expensive, large and present in only few facilities.
  • Therefore, I have set myself the goal to build a home training device for training the multifidus muscles.
  • The current project status can be found under prototype.
  • More background knowledge can be found under science.
